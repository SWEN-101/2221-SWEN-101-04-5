One of the main features of Pathways is the interactive map, where you can zoom in to your location to view the buildings around you.

On the left control panel of each screen, there is an option for "Emergency Contacts". This button will take you to the page with all options regarding emergency contacts, including creating contacts, accessing contacts, and alerting contacts.

On the left control panel of each screen, there is an option for "Weather". This button will take you to the page with all available information regarding weather, including forcast, clothing suggestions, and alerts.
